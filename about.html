About Me

This is what I do

In all honesty I'm just a Canadian university student who has taken an interest in cars. I also happen to really like learning about things and sharing what I learn. So, I started an Instagram page posting about cars, how they work, motorsports, and just about anything I think will be interesting and/or useful to others.

There is no real end goal for all this, just to find an enjoyable use for my interests.
